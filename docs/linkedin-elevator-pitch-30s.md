## Video Production Guide

### **Recording Setup:**
- **Camera:** iPhone 14 Pro or better (vertical 1080Ã—1920)
- **Lighting:** Ring light or natural window light (face well-lit)
- **Background:** Clean, professional (office or AI LUXE branded backdrop)
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders, centered
- **Eye Level:** Camera at eye height (not looking down)

### **Delivery Tips:**
- **Energy:** High energy, smile, confident
- **Pace:** Slightly faster than normal speech (urgency)
- **Pauses:** Brief pause after "25x ROI" and dollar amounts
- **Gestures:** Use hands to emphasize key points (but keep in frame)
- **Eye Contact:** Look directly at camera (not at screen)

### **Editing:**
- **Captions:** Auto-generate, bold key terms ("25x ROI", "$7,500")
- **B-Roll:** Insert 3-4 second screen recordings (0:08-0:18)
- **Music:** Upbeat, modern (low volume, 10%)
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts (0.2s) between sections
- **End Screen:** 2 seconds with logo + URL

### **Optimal Posting Times (LinkedIn):**
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM (Kuwait/Dubai time)
- **Avoid:** Weekends, late evenings
