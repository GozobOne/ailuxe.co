## Technical Specifications

### **Recording Settings (Loom)**

- **Quality:** 1080p HD

- **Frame Rate:** 30 fps

- **Microphone:** High-quality USB mic (Blue Yeti recommended)

- **Camera:** Off (screen-only recording)

- **Cursor:** Enabled with highlight (yellow circle)

- **System Audio:** Off (add music in post-production)

### **Browser Setup**

- **Browser:** Chrome (latest version)

- **Extensions:** Disable all except Loom

- **Zoom Level:** 100%

- **Window Size:** Full screen (1920Ã—1080)

- **Tabs:** Close all except AI LUXE

- **Notifications:** Disable system notifications

### **Lighting & Environment**

- **Room:** Quiet space, no background noise

- **Time:** Record during low-traffic hours (fewer interruptions)

- **Microphone Distance:** 6-8 inches from mouth

- **Pop Filter:** Use to reduce plosives (p, b, t sounds)
